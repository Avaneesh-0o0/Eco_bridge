# FoodBridge AI - Project Summary

## Overview

FoodBridge AI is a smart food rescue and redistribution platform designed to reduce food waste and combat food insecurity by connecting food donors with NGOs and volunteer networks in real time.

Every day, restaurants, hotels, marriage halls, corporate cafeterias, and event venues discard large quantities of perfectly edible food due to a lack of coordination and visibility. At the same time, NGOs and shelters struggle to meet the food requirements of vulnerable communities.

FoodBridge AI bridges this gap by creating an intelligent ecosystem where surplus food can be listed, matched, collected, and delivered before it becomes waste.

---

## Problem Statement

Food wastage and hunger coexist despite the availability of surplus food.

Current food donation processes are often:

* Manual and time-consuming
* Dependent on phone calls and messaging groups
* Difficult to coordinate in real time
* Inefficient for urgent food redistribution

As a result, valuable food resources are lost while communities continue to face food insecurity.

---

## Solution

FoodBridge AI provides a centralized platform where food donors can quickly publish surplus food availability.

The system then:

* Identifies nearby NGOs and volunteers
* Prioritizes donations based on urgency
* Matches food with suitable recipients
* Coordinates pickup and delivery
* Tracks donations and impact metrics

This transforms food donation from a manual process into a fast, transparent, and scalable food rescue network.
