# FoodBridge AI - Design Theme System

## Design Philosophy

FoodBridge AI should not feel like a government portal, NGO software, or CRUD application.

The application should feel:

* Premium
* Human-centered
* Hopeful
* Modern
* Trustworthy
* Impact-driven

The user should feel they are participating in a social movement, not filling forms.

# Emotional Goal

When a user opens FoodBridge AI, the feeling should be:

"I am helping save food and help people."

Not:

"I am managing records."

That emotional difference is what makes the product memorable.
